Welcome to Cove Sales Coach

Your real-time coaching assistant that listens to your calls and guides you through every step of the Cove security sales process.

Coaching suggestions, objection rebuttals, and script guidance -- all in real time.

Step 4: Coaching Suggestions

The SUGGESTION card shows exactly what to say next, pulled from the Cove sales script.

INTRO > DISCOVERY > INFO > BUILD > CLOSING

That's great to hear you just moved -- congratulations! I'm glad you decided to get a security system; it's a smart move. Let me ask you, have you ever had a security system before?

Step 5: Objection Handling

When a customer pushes back, the system instantly detects it and shows approved rebuttals.

OBJECTION DETECTED
Shopping Around
"I'm calling a few different companies to compare"
HOW TO RESPOND
I totally understand -- it's smart to compare. Just so you know, a lot of people compare us with the big names and end up choosing Cove because of the no-contract, the pricing, and the customer service. And with the 60-day trial, you can try it risk-free.
The rebuttal card pulses red to grab your attention instantly.

Covers: price concerns, spouse approval, shopping around, technician questions, monitoring, and more.
