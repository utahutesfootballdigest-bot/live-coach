Welcome to Cove Sales Coach

Your real-time coaching assistant that listens to your calls and guides you through every step of the Cove security sales process.

Coaching suggestions, objection rebuttals, and script guidance -- all in real time.

Step 1: Set Up Audio

Before each call, grant microphone access for your voice and share the customer's audio source (browser tab or app).

1 Click Grant Mic Access and allow your microphone
2 Click Share Audio Source and select where customer audio plays
3 Make sure to check "Share audio" in the dialog

Step 2: Live Transcript

The left panel shows a real-time transcript of the conversation. Your speech appears in teal and the customer's in light teal.

The transcript updates as you speak -- no typing needed. Just focus on the conversation.

Step 3: Stage Progress

The stage bar at the top tracks where you are in the call flow. It moves through each stage automatically as you follow the script.

1 Intro -- Greet and determine if new or existing customer
2 Discovery -- Why they want security, who's in the home
3 Info -- Collect name, phone, email, address
4 Build -- Build the system with doors, windows, cameras, etc.
5 Closing -- Present pricing, discounts, and close the sale

Step 4: Coaching Suggestions

The SUGGESTION card shows you exactly what to say next, pulled directly from the Cove sales script. Just read it naturally.

Suggestions update in real time based on what the customer says. The system tracks which questions have been asked so it never repeats.

Step 5: Objection Handling

When a customer pushes back, the system instantly detects the objection and shows you approved rebuttals with exact wording to use.

! Price concerns -- monthly vs upfront rebuttals
! Spouse approval -- 60-day trial angle
! Shopping around -- no-contract comparison
! Not sure they need it -- risk-free trial

Step 6: Customer Profile

The system automatically captures the customer's name, phone, email, and address as they speak. Equipment is tracked as you build the system.

You can click any field to manually correct it if the voice recognition got something wrong.

Step 7: Live Pricing

As you add equipment to the system, the pricing section updates in real time showing equipment costs, monthly monitoring, and any applicable discounts.

$ Toggle between Cove Plus and Cove Basic plans
$ Apply coupon codes for additional discounts
$ Equipment totals update automatically

Bonus: Practice Mode

Click Practice with AI from the setup screen to run practice calls with an AI customer. It simulates real scenarios with different personalities, objections, and home setups.

Practice mode uses the same coaching system as live calls, so you get the full experience without a real customer.

You're Ready!

That's everything you need to know. The Sales Coach is designed to make every call smoother, more consistent, and more confident.

Close this tutorial and click Start Live Session to begin your first coached call. Good luck!
